    Modern Astronomy, Fall 2024

    This course is an introduction to astronomy for non-majors at PKU. We will discuss galactic astronomy, including stars and exoplanets, galaxy structure and formation, the Big Bang and Cosmology, and our own Solar System. The syllabus is here. We do not use a textbook, but the free openstax textbook is an excellent resource.
